Our Facility

Amartya salon is a luxurious retreat that has established itself as the top beauty destination for discerning women in Bengaluru since its inception in 1987. Drawing inspiration from the legendary queen's beauty and power, this salon is a trailblazer in India, providing top-notch professional services in a contemporary yet regal atmosphere. Their main aim is to offer a pampering experience that helps every client feel like a queen, whether it's spending time with friends or taking some much-needed personal time. There's nothing quite like a rejuvenating session at Amartya's salon, which has earned the distinction of being the best salon in India.

Our Specialists

At Amartya's salon, our spacious and elegant interior exudes an air of sophistication fit for royalty. Our decor features a tasteful blend of luxurious shades, including leather black, delicate gold, and neutral cream, adorning our furniture and creating a charmingly feminine ambiance. Each workstation has been thoughtfully designed to evoke a sense of serenity and indulgence, promising a truly satisfying and unique experience for our beloved "Queens." From the moment you step inside, you'll feel enveloped in a calming atmosphere that's sure to leave you feeling pampered and refreshed.
